I'm Juli Brady

When I was 3 years old, I would jump from a stool through the air, across the room, into my dad's arms while he sat in his chair. Sometimes he would not be paying attention.
I think these past couple years have been the closest I have gotten to recreating that jump - the adult version.

Here I am in Nashville, trying to find my footing and "start over". I have spent the last ten years as a Civil/Environmental Engineer in California. I just jumped and landed in Nashville. I'm not sure if it is going to catch me or not.
I love to go fast, so anything with wheels typically works for me. I am often on my bicycle (or snowboarding at Shelby Park if it blizzards). Trail running is better than hiking. I also love beer, thus why I probably need to keep moving.
